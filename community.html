Community Guidelines

Introduction

We're dedicated to creating a space where everyone can connect with confidence. Our guidelines reflect our commitment to safety, consent, and honesty to ensure that this community is welcoming and empowering for all.

Core Principles

  • Safety Comes First: Harassment, abuse, or harmful behavior has no place here. We've implemented tools and policies to protect you.
  • Be Honest: Authenticity builds trust. Represent yourself truthfully, and engage with others in good faith.
  • Consent is Key: Respect boundaries and communicate clearly in all interactions. Always seek mutual agreement and understanding.
  • Accountability Matters: We take reports seriously. Users reported multiple times will be suspended pending a thorough review.

Moderation

If something makes you feel unsafe or uncomfortable, please don't hesitate to report it. Our Community Advocates review all reports confidentially. Users with multiple reports are automatically suspended while we investigate to ensure safety for everyone.

Your voice matters. By reporting inappropriate behavior, you're helping us create a stronger, safer community. Thank you for being an advocate for respect and care.
